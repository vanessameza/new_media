Who Am I?

I'm from Whittier, CA. Home of Whittier High School, where parts of Back to the Future were filmed! I love all creative outlets, whether that be coding or photography. I enjoy reading, learning, coding, photography and watching the Big Bang Theory. Bazinga! I LOVE Starbucks! I believe in continuous self-evolvement and surrounding myself with other positive people. My dream job is to be a web developer at a company that values their employees and where I can bring my skills and personality with me.
